COVID-19: The rise of COVID-19 cases and reported crime numbers in major cities.

While many law enforcement agencies predicted a rise in crime during the COVID-19 outbreak due to unemployment, closed businesses, increased demand on law enforcement, release of prisoners to reduce spread, and a number of other factors; in some US cities, this has not been the case. Data pulled from portals for the cities of Los Angeles, Chicago, and Atlanta and the New York Times regional COVID-19 database.
